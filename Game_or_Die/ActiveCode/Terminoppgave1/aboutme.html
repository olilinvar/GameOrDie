About me

Hi! My name is Oliver Lindtein.

I am a 16-year-old boy living in Oslo with my mother, father, little sister, little brother, two rabbits and a hamster. I was born in Beijing, China in 2006. I moved to Norway when I was 7 years old and have lived here since. I went to an elementary school (Smestad Skole) that I attended for 6 years before changing to a private school (Oslo Montessoriskole) to spend my last year in elementary school as well as finishing middle school and eventually starting in high school here at Kuben which I am writing this very sentence at. I am very interested in computers, software, networks, coding and more. I am eager to learn more about these fields. My dream is to work with either cybersecurity, AI, or space exploration. When it comes to my pros and cons, I sometimes have a difficult time with staying on task as I zone out on occasion, but on the other hand once something is within my field of interest, I suddenly become hyper focused with a high working speed while being creative and a little over the top engaged. I am great at reading people and I have an excellent social understanding but at the same time I dislike being social in long periods. I am great at adapting to my environment including the people and I constantly put others needs above mine. I have a strong sense of morality and justice. If I see someone being treated unfairly, I act and fight for what I think is right. My biggest weakness is being terrible at consistently sticking to routines but it as something I want to improve on and I am very dedicated to doing so.
